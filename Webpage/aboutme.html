As a young entrepreneur, I am passionate about contributing to innovation and progress in an ever-increasing complex and ambiguous world. My desire to constantly learn and broaden my mind is fostered across all areas of life; technology, economics, sustainability, entrepreneurship, health, culture, sports.

Over the past months it gradually became clear to me that I want to be a software engineer (check out why in my blog post). Founders and Coders appeared to me in mid June 2022 just a couple of weeks after I initially started to learn coding. Next to my full time job it was never easy to make enough time learning all the languages and tools from scratch and suffice the application requirements. But I am determined once I have a goal and frankly proud of what I learned so far.

Combining my mechanical engineering background, my experience and business development with profound coding skills will enable me to become a full product builder, eventually helping civilization to thrive and prosper.

Luckily I will no longer work full-time after August 2022 and what would better fit my plans than participating in the Founders and Coders Skills Bootcamp? Other than paid vacation I really don't know. Therefore I would be more than happy if you see the potential and opportunity as well.

Best Regards from Bali, Indonesia

Marlo Kürner
