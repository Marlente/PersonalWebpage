#MiniGamePlattform

Games are designed to learn coding: they demand to develop a logic but are super fun at the same time. The MiniGamePlattform offers three different games including Rock-Paper-Scissors, Magic Conch Shell and Number Guessing. On the page I show only one game at a time and hide the rest. The architecture is simple and the games are powered by plain JavaScript features.

#openAI

With the new possibilities of advanced AI tools I saw potential in building an interface to the 'davinci' engine released by openAI. The objective was to fine-tune the basemodel for writing research proposals to apply for government funding. Therefore I collected, prepared and evaluated data and trained the model with >100 key-value pairs. Further I developed a JS script, including an express server for my backend, as well as a simple UI. Not forget to mention all the economic investigations as I scaled my productivity 27-fold for less than 0.06$ per proposal.

Building time: 9 days

#Fruitshop

As part of an application process I built a small online fruitshop. The page enables the user to select out of different fruits in a shelf and add them to a shopping cart. Within the shopping cart it is possible to add/delete items as well as clear all items of one fruit sort. As an additional feature I added a calculation for the total price in JavaScript.
